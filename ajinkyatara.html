About Ajinkyatara Fort

Ajinkyatara Fort stands tall in the heart of Satara and holds great historical importance from the Maratha era. Built by Raja Bhoj and later strengthened by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the fort offers breathtaking views of Satara city, especially during sunrise and sunset.
