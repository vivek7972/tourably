About Koyna Dam

Koyna Dam is one of the largest dams in Maharashtra and a major source of hydroelectric power. Located in the Western Ghats near Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary, the dam is surrounded by dense forests, scenic viewpoints, and the serene Shivsagar Lake.
